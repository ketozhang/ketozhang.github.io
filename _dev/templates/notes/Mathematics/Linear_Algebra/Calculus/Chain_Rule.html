Scalar-Scalar

We want to take the derivative of a scalar composite function \(f​\) with respect to a scalar \(x​\). \[ \begin{align} f(x) &= f(g(x)) = f \circ g \circ x\\ \frac{\mathrm{d}}{\mathrm{d} x}f(x) &= \frac{\mathrm{d}f}{\mathrm{d} g}\frac{\mathrm{d}g}{\mathrm{d} x} \end{align} \]
