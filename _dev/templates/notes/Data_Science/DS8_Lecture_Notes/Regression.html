Residuals

Residuals from a linear regression always have: * Zero mean * Zero correlation with \(x'\) * Zero correlation with \(\hat y'\)

Fitted values \(\hat y\) from a linear regression always have: * 1 correlation with \(\hat y\) * Related to the correlation and variance of \(y\) by:

$$
\text{Var}[\hat y] = r^2 \cdot \text{Var}[y]
$$
