Kinetic Energy

General Definition
\[ \begin{align} K_\text{lin} &= \frac{1}{2}m\dot r^2\\ K_\text{rot}&=\frac{1}{2}I\dot\phi^2\\ \end{align} \]
  • \(I\) : Moment of inertia where, \(I=\int{Mr\;dR}\)

Potential Energy

Gravity
\[ \begin{align} U_g &= -\frac{GMm}{r} &\\ U_g &= -mgh \end{align} \]

\(g\) : Gravitational acceleration which, \(g=\frac{GM}{R^2}\) for a constant \(M\) and \(R\).

Hooke’s Law
