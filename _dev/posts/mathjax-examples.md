## Inline Math

Newton's second law in simple scalar form is $F=ma$.

## Display Math

Newton's second law in simple scalar form is,

$$
F = ma
$$

## Environment Math

### Equation

$$
\begin{equation}
    F = ma
\end{equation}
$$

### Align

$$
\begin{align}
    F &= ma\\
    \dot p &= m \dot a
\end{align}
$$

### Gather

$$
\begin{gather}
    F = ma\\
    \frac{\mathrm d p}{\mathrm d x} = m \frac{\mathrm d^2 x}{\mathrm d t^2}
\end{gather}
$$
