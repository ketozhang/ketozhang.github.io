Bio

I am (or am working on becoming) an astrophysicist and software engineer. My interest are in statistics research and technical development primarily in the field of astrophysics. My current role is at CalTech IPAC as a software developer working on the Nancy Grace Roman Space Telescope. Specifically, I am responsible for the software that will detect exoplanets through gravitational microlensing events.
